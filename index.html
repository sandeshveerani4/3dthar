EFFORT FROM YOUTH, AND FOR YOUTH

Lets be part of Career Counselling, Mental Health Seminars, and Informative Sessions.

3D Thar is a non profit organization, which aims to set sessions related to career counselling. We believe there is immense of talent in Tharparkar, but all they need is a little push in perfect direction.
Join us by volunteering.

Our Mission

Counselling

Our aim is to counsel the students of Thar about every different sector of career. We are aware about where the talented students of Thar lack, so we will try best to help them reaching their goals.

Sessions

Sessions are of great help if done in a right way. Our goal is to organize sessions in Tharparkar and invite students from all over Thar. We will also be inviting different career counselors from Thar and other regions.

Guidance

We are available on different social media platforms such as, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and WhatsApp. We are always available to guide students about every hurdle they face.
